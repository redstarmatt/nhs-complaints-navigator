Complaints involve vulnerable people in difficult situations. A paywall creates friction and erodes trust. By keeping the tool completely free, we maximise users, build credibility, and create valuable advertising inventory. ‚ö†Ô∏è Ethical Guardrails

  • Vulnerable audience: Users are often distressed. Ads must be helpful, not exploitative.
  • Solicitor vetting: Only SRA-regulated firms with verified clinical negligence track records. No "claims farmers" or ambulance-chasers.
  • Clear disclaimers: AI provides guidance, not legal advice. Users should seek professional advice for serious matters.
  • Free advocacy signposting: Always mention free NHS complaints advocacy services before/alongside paid alternatives.
  • No dark patterns: Easy to use tool without clicking ads. Revenue comes from genuine interest, not manipulation.
  • Data privacy: Medical documents processed transiently, not stored. Minimal data collection. GDPR compliant.

üîß Technical Note: AI Costs

At scale, AI API costs (Claude/GPT-4) could be ¬£0.01-0.05 per conversation. At 10k users doing ~3 AI interactions each = ¬£300-1,500/month in API costs. This is well within the revenue margin. Could reduce costs further by using smaller models for simple tasks (routing, template filling) and reserving larger models for complex analysis (response interpretation, document summarisation).
